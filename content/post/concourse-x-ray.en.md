*An experiment focused on exploring gameplay mechanics.*
<!--more-->

The premise is simple: we are an airport security guard and we must stop passengers from boarding their flights with forbidden items.

The final concept is genuinely fun, ending up as a sort of **Papers, Please** in 3D, with fewer bureaucratic procedures and more action. In **Concourse X-Ray**, waves of travelers arrive hiding all kinds of objects under their clothes.

Our mission is to inspect them using X-ray glasses that cover only part of the screen, while everything moves inside a small 3D environment. That adds tension to a situation that becomes increasingly confusing and hilarious as you try to keep track of the characters you have already checked and those you still have to inspect.

Will you manage to stop the smart ones trying to sneak forbidden objects under their clothes? Or will you lose control of the situation while attempting to inspect the stampede of travelers rushing to board the plane?
